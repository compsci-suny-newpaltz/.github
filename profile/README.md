## Welcome

The **Department of Computer Science** at SUNY New Paltz prepares students for success in the rapidly evolving world of technology. Located in the Hudson Valley, our department offers rigorous programs with small class sizes, hands-on project work, and close collaboration with industry partners.

---

## Our Programs

| Degree | Credits | Description |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| **B.S. in Computer Science** | ~120 | Technical depth with science/engineering emphasis |
| **M.S. in Computer Science** | 30 | Advanced study in ML, AI, cybersecurity & data science |
| **B.S./M.S. Accelerated Pathway** | Combined | Complete your master's in as few as 5 years |

### Focus Areas

- Machine Learning & AI
- Cybersecurity
- Data Structures & Algorithms
- Web & Database Development
- Software Engineering
- Systems Programming

---

## Facilities

Our department is housed in the state-of-the-art **Science Hall**, a 77,000 sq. ft. facility featuring:

- **Specialized Computer Labs** — Current hardware, operating systems, and development tools
- **[Hydra Lab](https://hydra.newpaltz.edu/)** — High-performance computing environment for students and researchers ([Dashboard](https://hydra.newpaltz.edu/dashboard))
- **Network & Security Lab** — Advanced computing facilities for cybersecurity research
- **3D Printing & Maker Spaces** — Via the Hudson Valley Advanced Manufacturing Center
- **GPU Clusters & Research Infrastructure** — Supporting AI/ML research and coursework
